Geoendevina

Click on the map to indicate the position of what is seen in the image.

Each game has 5 rounds of different images.
You have 60 seconds to place the mark.

Geoendevina is a game to test your visual and geographical knowledge of Catalonia.

  • In this game you will be presented with a series of five images of different places in Catalonia. Your task is to locate each image on the map by clicking on its location in less than 60 seconds.
  • At the end of the game you will see a summary with the distances between your locations and the correct positions.

Game based on Backdrop de www.tripgeo.com
